Our Vision

To be a partner and known institution in Microfinancing and other NGO services, not only in Luzon but in the entire Philippines, by providing continuous financial assistance, promoting food security and environmental awareness, for a better way of living of people with integrity, hardworking, and well-disciplined in handling finances while caring for Mother Earth.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help the common people increase their self-confidence by providing soft loans with low interest rates and longer repayment periods, involving them in taking care of the environment, and teaching them values such as integrity, cooperation, perseverance, planning, and saving for the future.
